What Is Leather?

Leathers are devided CHROME and VEGITABLE. This is method of classification by tanning. CHROME LEATHER is tanned by chrome ingredient. While VEGITABLE LEATHER is tanned by tannin ingredient that is plant component.

Relatively, CHROME LEATHER is cheap and have short production period than VEGITABLE one. And CHROME one is light, flexible and resistant to pollution and heat. But, the ingredient called CHROME have a bad influence to eco.
VEGITABLE LEATHER is expensive and have a long production period than CHROME one.(This period is about decuple than CHROME one.) Also VEGITABLE LEATHER is hard, stiff, heavy and susceptible to scratch and pollution with water, sweat and oil. However, VEGITABLE one can be AGING. AGING means that the VEGITABLE LEATHER become softer and darker than first time.
